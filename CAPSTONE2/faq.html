Frequently Asked Questions...

Can my order be delivered?

Yes. Delivery is available to Washington DC and Northern VA. For delivery orders please call the shop and we’ll set it up. Delivery fee varies based on location and time and starts at $15.

How much advance notice is required for orders?

We don't have rigid rules, it's a bakery not the IRS. More notice is always better. For pickup orders please give at least 1 day advance notice. For delivery orders we need a little more lead time so we can arrange delivery with a driver. We understand how things go, so if you need something last minute call the shop and we will try our best to make it happen.

Can I stop in the shop and pickup what I’m looking for?

Yes, stop in for anything! Or call ahead and choose your variety and we'll pack them up and put them aside for you. This way when we sell out of items you can still get yours.

What if I need to cancel an order?

Please call us to cancel your order. Any order canceled with 48 hours notice will receive a refund. Orders canceled with less than 48 hours notice can not be refunded.

I can’t find what I’m looking for – can you handle special requests?

We are not a nut-free bakery; all of our baked goods are prepared on premise.Our coconut cake, carrot cake, lemon bars, and chocolate-coconut cupcakes all contain coconut.

What is your allergy information?

Please call the shop or e-mail us at bakelefrance@gmail.com and tell us what you’d like. We will try our best to help you out!

How far in advance should I place my order?

Please place your orders 1-2 weeks in advance for a simple cake.
